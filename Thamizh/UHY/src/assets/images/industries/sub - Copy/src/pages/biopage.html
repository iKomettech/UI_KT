Diana McDougall

Regional President - Lowcountry, SC

Steve Stone serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of CoastalSouth Bancshares, Inc. and Coastal States Bank. As CEO, Steve led the recapitalization of CoastalSouth in 2017 and is responsible for executing the business strategy that he and Tony Valduga implemented when they joined the bank. That strategy is focused on strong organic growth through CSB’s community banking line of business and specialized lending groups combined with mergers and acquisitions. Prior to joining Coastal States in 2017, he previously served as Chief Strategy Officer and General Counsel at Community & Southern Bank. Steve is responsible for mergers and acquisitions, strategic planning and special projects, and investor relations. Steve was formerly an attorney in the Financial Services and Products Group at the law firm of Alston & Bird, LLP in Atlanta, Georgia. While at Alston & Bird, Steve’s practice was focused primarily on public and private mergers and acquisitions and corporate finance transactions in the financial services industry. Steve received a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Virginia and received his law degree from the Washington College of Law at The American University, cum laude.
