Request a Grant

We welcome grant requests that help support our local communities. Grant requests are initiated through the Commitment Grant Application. To learn more or complete your grant request,please click here. Instructions on where to send the grant request are included in the grant request document.

  • The Coastal States Commitment will be generally looking to make grants to validly established 501(c)3 charitable organizations working in the communities served by CSB.
  • The Commitment Advisory Board will meet to review grant requests on a scheduled quarterly basis; however, the Board may convene more frequently as needed from time-to-time to review expedited requests.
