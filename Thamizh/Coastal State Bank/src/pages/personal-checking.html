Important Information

  • Message and data rates may apply. Fees may apply for certain optional services available through Personal Online & Mobile Banking.
  • Coastal States Bank may pay overdrafts at our discretion and reserves the right to terminate this service at any time. You are required to pay a $31 overdraft fee for each overdraft transaction that is paid by the bank. There is a limit of $155 each day in total fees that you can be charged for overdrawing your account. Items not paid will be returned and you will incur a $31 returned item fee. Overdraft service applies to checks, pre-authorized electronic debits, and online banking and bill payment transactions. Additionally, ATM withdrawals and one-time debit card transactions are covered if you choose to opt in to this service for those transactions. Reimbursement of overdraft coverage payments must be made promptly by making a deposit in your account. Overdraft items are generally not covered for accounts that are not in good standing or do not have regular deposits.

  • Debit card and customer activation required.
  • Credit file monitoring may take several days to begin following activation.
  • Bill must be paid through the premier account for coverage to apply.
  • To reduce the monthly maintenance fee, enroll in eStatements ($2.00 reduction), maintain an average daily balance of $2,500 during the monthly statement cycle ($4.95 reduction) Eliminate the monthly maintenance fee by maintaining a combined average daily balance of $10,000 during the statement cycle in related Local.Checking or related Local.
