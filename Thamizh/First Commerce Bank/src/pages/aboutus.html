Our Story

Banking Built for Community

First Commerce Bank started with a community-first mindset, focused on serving and empowering local neighborhoods. Our founders saw the importance of having a bank focused on people, small businesses, and communities—and that vision still guides us today. We shape every decision, investment, and service to help you and our community thrive.

First Commerce is dedicated to relationships, providing people, businesses, and communities in our markets with high-quality products and services and superior customer service. Five Star Service Standards

First Commerce Bank, our Five-Star Service Standards define how we serve you every day:

1.Friendly

We greet our Customers with a warm and welcoming energy. Our Customers feel comfortable speaking to us and are uplifted after each interaction — they feel like friends and look forward to seeing us again.

2.Fast & Accurate

We have an eye for details and are efficient. We are mindful of completing tasks at a brisk pace, yet we don’t compromise the quality of our work. We masterfully balance speed and precision.

3.Find Solutions

We are eager to understand the problem. We consider many options, choose the best solution and ask for help when needed.

4.Follow-Through

We take ownership of tasks — completing all the necessary steps while collaborating with colleagues. We stay in communication with our Customers until the situation is resolved.

5.Fun!

We do fun things. We delight in making our Customers smile and laugh — creating a lighthearted environment, both within our bank, and within the communities we serve.
