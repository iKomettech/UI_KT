North Essex Chamber of Commerce Event

April 13, 2025

Share

First Commerce Bank was a proud sponsor of the Orthodox Jewish Chamber of Commerce Business Networking Event held at the Adventure Crossing USA Complex in Jackson. Attendees enjoyed food and fun interactive games, from the driving range to the simulated arcade games (XV/RV Games). A special thank you to John Crisafulli: VP, Senior Business Development Officer, Chris Smith: VP, Commercial Loan Officer, Marina Votinova: Lakewood Community Banking Officer and Danial Natoli: Lakewood Assistant Branch Manager. This is what hometown community banking is all about!
