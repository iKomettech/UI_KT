First Commerce Bank Person of the Week

Gregory Garcia

Mr. Gregory Garcia currently serves as EVP, Chief Operating Officer for First Commerce Bank, a community bank headquartered in Lakewood, NJ. Greg began his career with the Bank in 2021, serving as the Bank’s Chief Risk Officer and was promoted to COO in 2022. Prior to joining FCB, Greg served as Chief Financial Officer and Chief Operating Officer for William Penn Bank from 2019 to 2021. Greg began his career in community banking in 2004 as a consultant for the strategic consulting firm, FinPro, Inc. where he worked for 14 years and served as the firm’s Executive Managing Director.

Greg received a BA from Rutgers University.
