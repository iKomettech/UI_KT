Forge Your Path

Lowenstein’s unique Orientation, Onboarding, and Integration (OOI) department prepares lateral partners for immediate and long-term success with in-depth planning and support. The OOI team guides you through the logistics of moving to a new firm and creates—and helps execute on—a bespoke plan tailored to meet your individual needs and goals. The OOI program includes regular check-ins and feedback sessions to ensure that your transition is as smooth as possible and that you are integrating culturally as well. The firm’s leadership team is always available to provide guidance and support as you navigate your new role.

0-3MONTHS

Meet key attorneys and staff across departments to provide a working knowledge of how the firm operates.

  • Introductions across practices, firm leadership, and key business services departments
  • Review of immediate practice needs and any changes since initial recruitment discussions
  • Launch of client transfer process

4-8MONTHS

Nurture existing client relationships, identify cross-practice opportunities, and develop plans for targeting new prospects.

  • Creation/implementation of business development plan, including key marketing strategies and tactics
  • Identification of cross-practice opportunities with current Lowenstein clients
  • Professional development, trainings, and continuing legal education sessions

9-12MONTHS

Continue to nurture business opportunities and meet with key stakeholders across the firm; regroup with dedicated OOI team to discuss any additional assistance or support.

  • Execution of business development plan and marketing strategies/tactics, with reporting on key metrics/performance indicators
  • Continued check-ins and/or introductions across firm practices and offices
