FAQs

What Is Career Connect?

CareerConnect helps Paul, Weiss attorneys and alumni pursue in-house careers and jobs in the public and nonprofit sectors. We encourage you to use this tool to support your career development. CareerConnect also helps our clients and alumni fulfill recruitment needs by connecting them to our amazing network of former and current Paul, Weiss attorneys.

I Am Looking for a Job

How do I apply for a featured opportunity?

Each opportunity on the job board can be expanded by clicking on the job title. Each position includes “How to Apply” details.

Alumni who encounter any problems using the site should contact careerconnect@paulweiss.com.

How do I search for jobs?

CareerConnect’s “Search Jobs” tool helps you refine available opportunities by job title, keywords, country and industry.

If I am being considered for a position, how will I be notified?

Responses to applications are solely conducted by the employers. Submitted applications are not shared with Paul, Weiss, and the firm is not involved with any employment decisions made by any entities posting jobs on CareerConnect.

I Am Searching for a Candidate

How do I post a job opportunity on CareerConnect?

Click “Submit a Job” to complete the submission form. You can also submit a job post by emailing careerconnect@paulweiss.com.

Please note that Paul, Weiss retains full discretion as to whether a job can be posted to CareerConnect. Paul, Weiss reserves the right to remove any posting at any time.

How do I edit or remove a job opportunity?

Contact careerconnect@paulweiss.com to request any edits or removals.

Will I receive a notification if my job posting is approved?

If you submitted the job opportunity via the submission form or sent all necessary job information to careerconnect@paulweiss.com, a status notification will be sent to the contact listed in the submission after its review.

Who has access to CareerConnect?

Current Paul, Weiss attorneys and alumni can access CareerConnect.

Applications received via CareerConnect do not indicate any endorsement or certification by Paul, Weiss.
