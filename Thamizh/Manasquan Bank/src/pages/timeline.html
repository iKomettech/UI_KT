  • Timeline invert

    March 21, 1874

    Incorporated and chartered by the State of New Jersey as the Squan Village Building & Loan Association. The Association was located in the Borden building in Manasquan until 1922.

  • Timeline invert

    February 10, 1910

    The Association re-incorporated, changing the name to Manasquan Building & Loan Association.

  • Timeline invert

    1923

    The Association moved to the Tassini Building on Main Street, Manasquan, then to the new Tassini Building at 129 Main Street and remained there until 1936.

  • Timeline invert

    1932

    The Association acquired property located at 185 Main Street upon which a house was being utilized as a restaurant.

  • Timeline invert

    June 12, 1936

    The Association built its first permanent quarters at 185 Main Street, Manasquan.
