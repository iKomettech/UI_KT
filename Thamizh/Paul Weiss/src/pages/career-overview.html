The Summer Associate Experience

When you summer with us, we take every opportunity to show you why your work matters and how it will make a difference. You will have the opportunity to build and shape your own summer experience through substantive assignments, robust training, multifaceted mentorship, client engagement and social events

2L Summer Program

Each year we participate in on-campus interview programs and resume collections at the nation’s most competitive law schools. We also encourage qualified applicants to apply directly via our online application portal.

Learn More and Apply

Meaningful Assignments

Summer associates don’t just work on discrete assignments, they are immersed in their matters and are integral members of their teams. As a summer associate you will:

  • Work directly on high-profile, transformative deals; industry-shaping litigations; and one-of-a-kind restructurings
  • Be able to select assignments from various practice groups and subject matters as well as have opportunities to work on a broad range of impactful pro bono matters
  • Have opportunities to sit in on negotiations, depositions, conference calls, court hearings and more

2L Pauli Murray Fellowship

At Paul, Weiss, diversity, equity and inclusion define our culture. In celebration of the varied perspectives and backgrounds that enrich our profession and help us solve problems more effectively, we proudly offer the Pauli Murray Fellowship.

Mentorship & Inclusion

Summer associates don’t just work on discrete assignments, they are immersed in their matters and are integral members of their teams. As a summer associate you will:

  • Paired with a partner mentor and an associate mentor
  • Encouraged to join firmwide Networks (Asian-Pacific Lawyers, Black Lawyers, Family, Latin Lawyers, Pride @ Work and Women)
  • Invited to a variety of panel discussions, Network events, trainings and more, providing access to additional layers of support and mentorship

Training & Career Development

We are committed to our summer associates’ learning and development. As a summer associate you will:

  • Receive intensive training in a mix of practical skills workshops and more traditional classroom-style presentations
  • Learn about diverse areas of law and practical legal skills, such as performing due diligence on an M&A transaction; writing effectively; clarifying commonly used business jargon; managing assignments and adding value as a junior associate on your matters; and developing real-world advocacy writing and public speaking skills
  • Benefit from individualized coaching in areas such as presentation and writing skills

Client Engagement

From day one, summer associates gain insights into client service at the highest level. As a summer associate you will:

  • Have opportunities to join client calls and meetings as well as to work directly with clients, under the supervision of our attorneys
  • Hear unique perspectives from clients, as well as legal and subject matter experts, through exclusive fireside chats, dialogues and presentations

Social Opportunities

We recognize the importance of recharging and socializing and provide plenty of opportunities for you to do so. As a summer associate you will:

  • Connect with your fellow summer associates, firm associates, counsel and partners through meals, receptions, events at iconic venues within each location, sporting events, and more
  • Have access to personal and group wellness activities, benefits and resources

Our Summer Program Locations

Our summer program is based out of our New York; Los Angeles; San Francisco; Washington, D.C.; Wilmington; and Toronto offices. During the program, summer associates experience what it is really like to work at Paul, Weiss, immersing themselves in each office’s key practice areas and getting to know the lawyers at each location.

Summer associates at all of our offices will participate in a variety of fantastic social events, including at iconic venues within each location. Past events have included Shakespeare in the Park, tours of the capital’s landmarks and trips to wine country. There are also opportunities for summer associates from all of our offices to attend trainings and marquee social events at our New York office.

Learn more about our summer program offices, including our market-leading practices and the elite team of lawyers you will work alongside in each location:

  • New York

    Our New York office serves as the firm’s headquarters, with nearly 800 lawyers working across all of the firm’s core practice capabilities: corporate, litigation, restructuring, tax, executive compensation, real estate and personal representation. Since 1875, the firm has been a leader in the New York legal world, playing a key role in transformational transactions and landmark litigation. Longtime clients include many of the city’s iconic companies and financial institutions. The office is home to members of Paul, Weiss’s market-leading transactional practices, including M&A, private equity, investment funds, finance and capital markets, among others, as well as many of our renowned litigators, including elite trial lawyers, nationally prominent white collar and regulatory defense lawyers, and preeminent securities litigation lawyers.
