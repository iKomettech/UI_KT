2L Summer Program

Each year we participate in on-campus interview programs and resume collections at the nation’s most competitive law schools. We also encourage qualified applicants to apply directly via our online application portal.

Program Description

Our summer program offers an exciting opportunity to work on significant, headline-generating matters while experiencing the unique Paul, Weiss culture. Summer associates will be integrated into the firm through substantive assignments, robust training, multi-faceted mentorship, client engagement and social events.

Application Process

Applications for 2L positions in New York; Los Angeles; San Francisco; Washington, D.C.; and Wilmington, are now open. Applicants will need to provide the following documents:

  • Resumé
  • Cover letter indicating departmental interests
  • Unofficial law school transcript

Salary

Paul, Weiss summer associates, including Pauli Murray Fellows, are paid an annualized salary of $225,000, prorated for the number of weeks worked.
