Description

Our VentureCrushFG Pod program is both virtual and in-person (2 virtual pods + 1 in-person). Each Pod has 10-15 startups and 4-5 Pod leads (VCs + 2 from our VentureCrush team). Pods meet 4x during a 5-month period for off-the-record discussions. They also (optionally) attend 4 larger “on the record” VentureCrush sessions. We embrace #diversityequityinclusion

VentureCrushFG does NOT:

  • take equity,
  • charge
  • require anyone to do business with anyone else
  • promote competition between founders, or
    have a demo day (we do NOT stack rank our startups, require them to seek funding, dictate when they seek funding, or on what terms).
