Home Rent vs. Buy Calculator

Should you rent or should you buy your home? This calculator helps you weed through the fees, taxes and monthly payments to help you decide between these two options. This report is based on the original purchase price, fees and taxes payable at that time. Insurance and tax costs can fluctuate from year to year.
