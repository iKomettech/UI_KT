Net Worth Calculator

Your net worth is the value of all your assets, minus the total of all your liabilities. Put another way, it is what you own minus what you owe. If you owe more than you own, you have a negative net worth. If you own more than you owe, you have a positive net worth. This calculator helps you determine your net worth and estimates how it could grow (or shrink) over the next 10 years.
