U.S. Easy Tax Estimator

This is a simplified tax calculator if you don't need to all of the complexity of the full 1040 tax form. Simply select your tax filing status and enter a few other details to estimate your total taxes. Based on your projected tax withholding for the year, the tool calculates your refund or the amount you may owe the IRS next April.
