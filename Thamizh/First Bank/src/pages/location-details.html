Patrick Martins

VP/Branch Manager

(973) 895-5824

Patrick Martins is the VP/Branch Manager located at our Flemington Branch on Route 31. He is responsible for managing daily branch operations, sales strategies, team coaching, and community involvement.

Patrick has been in banking for 25 years, all of which were spent in his current field. Prior to joining first bank, he was a Branch Manager at Northfield Bank & PNC Bank, and an Assistant Branch Manager at TD Bank.

Mr. Martins has served as the Treasurer for Friends of Samuel Fleming House, and Member of the 200 Presidents Club. Patricio is fluent in Portuguese, Spanish, and French.
