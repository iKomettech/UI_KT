Q : Uniting for Ukraine?

A : Parole is neither a visa nor refugee status. For people who may otherwise be ineligible to enter the United States (e.g., unable to get a visa), parole is permission to remain in the United States for a temporary period because of a compelling emergency, urgent humanitarian reason, or significant public benefit. Parole requests for these reasons are often called Humanitarian Parole.

Q : Requests for Humanitarian Parole at Ports of Entry (Northern and Southern Border)?

A : Ukrainian Citizens may be denied parole at ports of entry (borders) as of April 25, 2022.
